# Agency OS — Breakout Videos (Post-Booking Objection Busters)

> **Format:** 1-3 min each, talking head, same environment as VSL
> **When shown:** On the call prep/confirmation page after booking
> **Purpose:** Handle objections before the call so closer doesn't have to
> **Total:** 10 videos

---

## Video 1: "What Results Can I Expect In The First 90 Days?"

Month one — we audit everything, install the Growth OS, get tracking in place, and launch the first campaigns. You'll see leads coming in by week three.

Month two — pipeline is filling. We're testing what works, killing what doesn't, and optimizing the entire flow from ad to booked appointment. Most clients see their calendar noticeably fuller.

Month three — the system is dialed. Lead flow is predictable. Your team knows what's coming in next week, next month. You stop guessing and start planning.

Will I promise you a specific number? No. Every business is different. But I will tell you — if you have a service people are already buying, and we install the right system, the math works. We've seen clients double their booked appointments in sixty days. But I'd rather under-promise and let you see it yourself.

## Video 2: "How Is This Different From Other Agencies?"

Most agencies do one thing. They run your ads, send you a report, and disappear until next month's invoice. They don't touch your CRM. They don't fix your follow-up. They don't care if the leads they send you actually become clients.

We're not an agency in the traditional sense. We're an operator. We install the full system — from the ad to the booked call to the signed contract — and then we run it for you. We sit in your Slack. We know your numbers. We own the outcome, not just the deliverables.

The other difference — you get senior talent. Not a twenty-two year old managing your account while the person who sold you the deal moves on. The person who builds your system is the person who operates it.

---

## Video 3: "I've Been Burned By Agencies Before"

I hear this on almost every call. "I spent ten grand with an agency and got nothing." Or "they promised thirty leads a month and I got a bunch of people who wouldn't pick up the phone."

Here's the thing — most agencies solve the wrong problem. They generate leads. But leads without a system to convert them are just a list of people who'll never become clients. If there's no automated follow-up, no nurture sequence, no appointment booking flow, and no tracking — the leads are worthless. That's not a lead problem. That's an infrastructure problem.

That's exactly what we fix. We don't just send you leads. We build the entire machine that turns attention into appointments and appointments into revenue. And you can see every step of it in real time.

---

## Video 4: "Can't I Just Hire Someone In-House?"

You can. A senior marketing hire is a hundred twenty to a hundred eighty thousand a year plus benefits. It takes three to six months to ramp. And they usually only know one or two channels — maybe paid ads, maybe SEO, but rarely both.

With us, you get a full-stack team — ads, funnels, email, CRM automation, analytics — for a fraction of that cost. And if it's not working, you cancel. Try that with a W-2.

The other thing — an in-house hire works inside your existing system. The problem is you don't have a system. We build the system first, then operate it. That's a fundamentally different thing.

## Video 5: "I Don't Have Time To Manage Another Agency"

That's the whole point. You don't manage us. We manage your growth.

You get a fifteen-minute weekly update and a shared dashboard you can check anytime. That's it. We handle everything — the campaigns, the follow-up, the optimization, the reporting. You focus on delivering your service and closing the deals we put in front of you.

Most founders we work with were spending ten-plus hours a week on marketing they shouldn't have been doing. We give that time back.

## Video 6: "How Much Does This Cost?"

Five to twenty-five thousand a month depending on scope. If we're running one channel and building a simple funnel, it's on the lower end. If we're managing paid ads across multiple platforms, building full CRM automation, running outreach, and operating the entire growth engine — it's on the higher end.

But here's how to think about it. If you're a service business doing a million a year and your average client is worth five, ten, twenty thousand dollars — how many new clients per month does the system need to generate to pay for itself? Usually it's one or two. Everything after that is profit.

We don't sell marketing. We sell infrastructure that produces clients predictably. The ROI math either works or it doesn't — and we'll show you the math on the call before you spend a dollar.

## Video 7: "What Does The Program Actually Include?"

Week one — full audit. We go through your numbers, your funnel, your ads, your CRM, your follow-up process. Everything.

Week two — Growth OS blueprint. We design the system custom to your business. Which channels, what messaging, what the funnel looks like, how follow-up works.

Weeks three and four — we build and launch. By day thirty, your Growth OS is live and generating pipeline.

After that — we operate it month over month. Campaign management, optimization, reporting, CRM management, follow-up automation. You get a senior operator who owns the number.

You don't build anything. You don't manage anything. You answer our questions during the audit and approve the strategy. That's your job. We do the rest.

## Video 8: "What If I Already Have A Marketing Team?"

Even better. We don't replace your team — we give them the operating system to execute better.

Most in-house teams are good at tactics but don't have the infrastructure. The tracking, the SOPs, the reporting loops, the attribution model — that's what we install. Your team gets better. They get clearer data. They know what's working and what isn't. And they have a system to follow instead of guessing.

We've worked with businesses that had two or three marketing people already. The problem was never the people — it was the lack of a system.

## Video 9: "What Happens On The Strategy Call? Is This A Sales Pitch?"

No. It's an audit.

We're going to look at how your business currently gets clients. Where the leads come from. What happens after someone raises their hand. How follow-up works. What your close rate looks like. Where people drop off.

Then we'll show you where the gaps are and what a Growth OS would look like for your specific situation.

If it's a fit, we'll talk about working together. If it's not, you walk away with a free audit and a clear picture of what's broken. We've told people on calls "you don't need us — here's what to fix yourself." That happens. No pressure. No games.

## Video 10: "Do You Guarantee Results?"

I guarantee the work, the system, and the reporting. I do not guarantee a specific number of clients — and anyone who does is lying to you.

What I can tell you: if you have a service people are already buying, and the only reason you're not growing faster is because you don't have a system to bring in more of those people — we will build that system. We will operate it. And you will see the difference.

If after ninety days the numbers don't make sense, we'll tell you exactly why and what to fix. Or you cancel. No long-term contracts. No hostage situations.

Rich people understand risk. This is a calculated bet with a high probability of working — not a guarantee. And that's exactly how we present it.
