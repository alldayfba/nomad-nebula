# DFY Closing Script — $20K Done-For-You Amazon Business
> For warm prospects who asked for DFY. 25-30 min call.
> Deck: https://dfy-pitch-deck.vercel.app

---

## 1. OPEN (3 min)
> "Yo [Name], what's good bro. Good to finally chop it up — you've been rocking with me for a minute now right?"

Let him talk. Build rapport. Keep it real — he's been following you 3-4 years.

> "So you mentioned you wanted the done-for-you option. I wanna walk you through exactly what that looks like, what we handle, what you handle, and the investment. Then if it makes sense we get you started. Cool?"

---

## 2. QUICK QUALIFY (3 min)

> "Before I get into it — just so I'm not wasting your time or mine — the DFY is a real investment. Outside of the program fee, you're gonna need capital for inventory too. Roughly how much do you have set aside for this?"

**If $20K+:** Perfect. Keep going.
**If under $20K:** Route to $10K coaching instead. "Let me show you the coaching option — same system, you just run it yourself with our support."

> "And time-wise — you know the DFY means we handle execution, but you're still gonna be on coaching calls learning the business. Can you do a few hours a week for that?"

---

## 3. THE PRE-FRAME (2 min)

> "Real talk — you've been watching me for like 3-4 years. What's actually stopped you from jumping in until now?"

**LISTEN.** Whatever he says = the objection coming later. Address it NOW:

- **"Money/timing"** → "That's exactly why the structure is the way it is — $10K to start, second $10K only after we've made you $100K. You're not risking $20K blind."
- **"Didn't know where to start"** → "That's literally why DFY exists. You don't start — we start for you."
- **"Wasn't sure if it was real"** → "You've watched me for years. You've seen the results. At some point watching becomes more expensive than doing."

---

## 4. SCREEN SHARE THE DECK (8 min)

> "Let me show you exactly what this looks like."

Share: **https://dfy-pitch-deck.vercel.app** — press F for fullscreen.

**Slide 1 (Cover):** "Done-for-you Amazon business. We build it, we run it, you profit."

**Slide 2-3 (Problem/Shift):** Quick — don't dwell. He already knows this.
> "You know the game — most people buy a course, get stuck, quit. The DFY flips that. We do the work."

**Slide 4 (What You Get):** Slow down here.
> "Two things happening at once. One — my team runs your entire Amazon business. Sourcing, listings, inventory, account management, all of it. Two — you get 1-on-1 coaching with me so you actually understand your own business. It's not a black box."

**Slide 5 (Team):** 
> "This is who's operating your account. Me, plus my coaches who are doing $20K-$50K a month on Amazon right now. Not teaching from 2020 — selling today."

**Slide 6 (System):** Walk through the 5 phases quickly.
> "Phase 1 we set everything up. Phase 2 we source your first products. Phase 3 we launch. Phase 4 we scale. Phase 5 we hit $100K and you decide if you want us to keep running it or take over."

**Slide 7 (AI):** 
> "And we're using AI tools I built — scanning 300+ retailers, finding products other sellers will never see. That's your edge."

**Slides 8-9 (Results):** Let the screenshots do the talking.
> "These are coaching results — people doing the work themselves. Imagine what happens when my team is the one executing."

---

## 5. PRICE DROP (3 min)

**Slide 10 (Investment):** Say it clean. No flinching.

> "$20,000. Here's how it works — $10,000 to get started. That covers setup, first products sourced, first inventory ordered, everything to get you live on Amazon."

> "Second $10,000 — you only pay that AFTER we've generated $100,000 in revenue for you. Half our fee is tied to your results. We don't eat unless you eat."

**Pause. Let it breathe.**

> "After $100K, you've got two options. Keep us running everything at 10% of profits — so if you profit $15K in a month, we take $1,500. Or you take over yourself — by that point you'll know the business inside and out from the coaching."

**Slide 11 (Why This Works):** Only if needed.
> "Your account. Your business. Your profits. We just operate it."

---

## 6. CLOSE (3 min)

> "Based on everything — does this feel like the right move?"

**If yes:** 
> "Let's get you started. Credit card or Zelle?"

Process payment. Don't keep talking. Don't celebrate on the call. Stay calm.

> "You're in. I'll send you the onboarding questionnaire tonight — takes 10 minutes. Then we schedule your kickoff call this week where we map out your first 90 days. One thing — don't buy any inventory or tools before that call. We plan it together first."

**If "let me think":**
> "What specifically do you need to think about?"

Then isolate and address. One shot. If he's still not ready:

> "No pressure bro. Let's lock in a time to reconnect — not 'sometime,' a real date. When works?"

**If he asks about the percentage again:**
> "10% of profits. Not revenue — profits. So if you do $50K revenue and profit $15K, that's $1,500 to us. And it's optional — you can take over anytime."

**If he tries to negotiate price:**
> "The coaching program is $10K and you do everything yourself. The DFY is $20K and we do everything for you. The second $10K doesn't even hit until after $100K. That's the structure."

Don't budge. Don't offer discounts. The performance tie IS the concession.
