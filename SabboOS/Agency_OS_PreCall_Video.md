# Agency OS — Pre-Call Prep Video

> **Runtime:** ~2 minutes
> **Format:** Talking head, same environment as VSL
> **When shown:** After someone books a strategy call — on the confirmation/prep page
> **Purpose:** Increase show rate, set expectations, build trust

---

## Script

Hey — you just booked a strategy call with us. Here's what you need to know.

This is not a pitch. This is an audit. We're going to look at your business and figure out where the growth bottleneck actually is. Most service business owners think it's a traffic problem. Usually it's a systems problem — and there's a massive difference.

Here's what to have ready for the call:

Your current monthly revenue — rough is fine. Where your clients come from right now — referrals, ads, organic, word of mouth. What you're spending on marketing, if anything. And how many new clients you need per month to hit your goals.

On the call, we'll walk through your current acquisition, conversion, and retention. We'll show you where the leaks are — where leads are falling off, where follow-up is breaking, where you're leaving money on the table. And if it makes sense to work together, we'll talk about what the Growth OS looks like installed in your specific business.

If it's not a fit — you still walk away with a free audit and a clear picture of what's broken. No pressure. No games.

See you on the call.
