## CONTENT CREATION WORKFLOW (Daily)

### Your Daily Time Investment: 2-3 Hours
```
Morning (1 hour):
- Review yesterday's metrics (views, engagement, calls booked)
- Film 1 piece of content (or batch film 2-3 on filming days)

Afternoon (30 min):
- Review editor's work, approve or request changes
- Post to Instagram stories (behind the scenes, Q&A, polls)

Evening (30 min):
- Engage with comments on YouTube and Instagram
- DM anyone who engaged meaningfully
- Quick EOD check: team filled out forms?
```

### Batch Filming Days (2x/month):
- Film 4-6 YouTube videos in one day
- Send all to editor
- Editor delivers 2/week
- You approve and schedule
