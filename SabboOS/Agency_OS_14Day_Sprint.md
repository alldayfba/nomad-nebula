## SPRINT OVERVIEW

**Objective:** Generate $120K-$200K+ in closed revenue in 14 business days through disciplined outbound execution, structured sales process, and progressive team ramp.

**Offer:** Done-For-You Growth Operating System — $5K-$25K/mo retainers (avg deal $15K)
**ICP:** 7-8 figure founders with product-market fit, burning ad spend without systems
**Close target:** 8-14 deals at $15K average = $120K-$210K

---

## TEAM ROSTER

| Role | Person | Days Active | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CEO / Lead Closer | Sabbo | Days 1-14 | Closes all calls Days 1-5, splits Days 6-14 |
| Sales Manager | Sabbo (initially) | Days 1-14 | Promotes top performer or hires by Day 7 |
| Setter #1 | New hire | Days 1-14 | Just onboarded, intelligent, bought in |
| Closer #1 | Existing closer | Days 1-14 | Shadows Days 1-5, solo Days 6-14 |
| Setter #2 (Optional) | TBD | Days 7-14 | Hire decision made Day 7 based on Setter #1 performance |

## CONTINGENCY PLAYS

### Contingency A: Behind Target at Day 7 (< $60K cumulative)

**Diagnosis:** Not enough conversations converting to bookings, or not enough calls being taken.

**Actions:**
1. **Setter overtime:** Push to 200+ touches/day immediately (Hormozi Rule of 100 = minimum, not maximum)
2. **Sabbo takes maximum calls:** Cancel all non-revenue meetings, stack calendar with 5-6 calls/day
3. **Launch ads early:** Don't wait for Day 9. Start $50/day immediately on best-performing angle
4. **Lower booking friction:** Remove application form, go direct to calendar booking
5. **Offer incentive:** First 3 clients get month 1 at $10K instead of $15K (still profitable)
6. **Personal outreach:** Sabbo sends 20 personalized Loom videos to ideal prospects
7. **Network activation:** Text/email every founder friend: "We're launching something. Know anyone scaling who needs growth infrastructure?"

### Contingency B: Behind Target at Day 10 (< $120K cumulative)

**Diagnosis:** Pipeline exists but not converting to cash. Either close rate, show rate, or deal size issue.

**Actions:**
1. **Close rate issue:** Sabbo takes over all closing (closer may need more training). Review call recordings for specific breakdown point
2. **Show rate issue:** Add pre-call Loom from Sabbo ("Looking forward to our call, here's what I found about your business..."). Call prospects 30 min before to confirm
3. **Deal size issue:** Stop offering monthly retainer as lead option. Push annual or 6-month commitment with discount. "Most clients invest $15K-$25K/mo for 6 months"
4. **Pipeline issue:** Emergency outreach blitz — setter does 250+ touches/day for final 4 days
5. **Offer issue:** If consistent objection pattern, adjust positioning mid-sprint. "What if we guaranteed X result in 90 days?"
6. **Scale ads:** Push ad spend to $200-$300/day on winning angle. Inbound leads have higher close rates

### Contingency C: Ahead of Target (> $150K at Day 10)

**Diagnosis:** Strong execution. Don't let off the gas.

**Actions:**
1. **Push for stretch goal:** $250K or $300K. Announce to team, create bonus pool
2. **Raise prices:** Test $20K-$25K positioning on all remaining calls
3. **Hire Setter #2 immediately** if not already done
4. **Scale ads to $200-$300/day** on all winning angles
5. **Begin planning Sprint 2** with larger team and higher targets
6. **Capture testimonials/case studies** from closed clients for Sprint 2 social proof

## KEY PRINCIPLES (Post This On The Wall)

1. **Traffic is the bottleneck** (Ben Bader). Everything else is downstream of volume. If pipeline is thin, the answer is always more touches.

2. **Rule of 100** (Hormozi). 100 primary actions per day, 100 days. Volatility = insufficient volume. If results are inconsistent, do more.

3. **The schedule is the schedule** (Sales Manager Playbook). No deviations. No "I'll skip training today." The routine creates the results.

4. **Call leads within 60 seconds** (Hormozi). Speed to lead is non-negotiable. Inbound leads from ads get called immediately.

5. **One focus per day** (Sales Manager Playbook). Not three. Not five. ONE thing the team works on improving. Connect it to yesterday's data.

6. **Celebrate wins loudly** (Hormozi). Make a big deal out of closes. Ring the bell. Call out names. Winning energy is contagious.

7. **Numbers are facts, not judgments** (Sales Manager Playbook). State them clearly. Never shame in public. Coach in private.

8. **Systems before ads** (Agency OS). We don't launch ads until the sales infrastructure works. Days 1-8 prove the model, Day 9 adds fuel.

9. **Close rate 30-40% = appropriately priced** (Hormozi). If closing above 50%, raise prices. If below 25%, fix the sales process first.

10. **One client can change your life** (Ben Bader). Every single call is an opportunity. Treat it that way.
