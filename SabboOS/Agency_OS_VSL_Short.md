# Agency OS — Short VSL Script (Service-Based Businesses)

> **Runtime:** ~4 minutes
> **Format:** Talking head, clean premium environment, direct eye contact
> **Framework:** Jeremy Haynes 6-Step + Step 0 (Summary Section)
> **Audience:** Service-based business owners doing $1M+/yr (real estate, legal, medical, home services, consulting)
> **Tone:** Direct, operator-to-operator, no hype, rich-people messaging
> **Rich People Rules Applied:** State price upfront, no income claims, no guarantees, no value stacks, use words like capital/infrastructure/P&L, speak with authority not hype

---

## [2:00–2:50] STEP 3: BUYING MOTIVES

The service business owners who work with us all share the same three problems.

First — they're tired of relying on referrals. Referrals are great, but you can't scale referrals. You can't tell your team "we'll have twelve new clients next month" if you're waiting for someone to mention your name at a dinner party.

Second — they've wasted money on marketing that didn't connect to revenue. They ran ads that got clicks but not clients. They hired an agency that sent a report full of impressions and reach — but the phone didn't ring. The problem wasn't the ads. The problem was there was no system between the ad and the signed contract.

Third — they're the bottleneck. Every new client comes through them personally. They're doing the sales, the follow-ups, the proposals, and the actual service delivery. They need a machine that generates and converts leads without them being involved in every step.
