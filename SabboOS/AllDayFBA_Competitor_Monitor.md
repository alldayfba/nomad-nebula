## HOW TO USE VIDIQ FOR COMPETITOR MONITORING

### Setup (One Time):
1. Install VidIQ Chrome extension
2. Go to each competitor's YouTube channel
3. VidIQ will show outlier scores on each video

### Weekly Scan (15 minutes):
1. Open YouTube
2. Visit each Tier 1 competitor's channel
3. Look for videos with **VidIQ outlier score of 3x or higher**
4. For each outlier, note:
   - Video title
   - Thumbnail style
   - View count vs. their average
   - Topic/angle
   - How you could adapt it for AI Amazon

### Monthly Analysis (30 minutes):
1. Review all outliers collected that month
2. Identify patterns: what TOPICS are getting outlier views across multiple creators?
3. Plan 2-3 videos based on proven topics
4. Check Tier 2 competitors for strategy shifts

### Tracking Sheet:
Create a simple Google Sheet:

## RED FLAGS TO WATCH FOR

Alert Sabbo immediately if any competitor:
1. **Starts using "AI" in their positioning** — your first-mover window is closing
2. **Launches a new mechanism name** — could steal market attention
3. **Gets a massive viral video** (1M+ views) — study it immediately
4. **Changes pricing dramatically** — could shift market expectations
5. **Gets negative press/lawsuits** — opportunity to counter-position against them
6. **Partners with a major tool** (SellerAmp, Keepa, etc.) — consider similar partnerships

## OPPORTUNITY ALERTS

Things to watch for that could benefit you:
1. **AI tool launches** — new tools = new content opportunities + potential partnerships
2. **Amazon policy changes** — changes create uncertainty = people seek coaches
3. **Competitor drama** — audience migration opportunities
4. **Trending topics** — AI, recession-proofing, side hustles trending = ride the wave
5. **New platforms** — new social platforms = first-mover content advantage
