## PRESENTER NOTES (For Closers Using This Deck on Calls)

### When to Use This Deck
1. **Screen share during closing calls** — walk through slides 1–14 as you present the offer (slides 4–8 during the pitch section of the script, slides 10–13 during the close)
2. **Post-call leave-behind** — send as PDF after "I need to think about it" or "I need to talk to my partner"
3. **Application follow-up** — send to qualified applicants before their call to warm them up
4. **VSL landing page** — content maps 1:1 to the VSL script sections

### Deck-to-Script Alignment

| Deck Section | Script Section | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Slide 2 (Problem) | Problem Agitation | 18:00–25:00 |
| Slide 3 (AI Shift) | Bridge to solution | ~25:00 |
| Slides 4–8 (System + Pillars + AI) | Present the Solution (Pitch) | 25:00–38:00 |
| Slide 9 (Bonus) | End of pitch, before price | ~37:00 |
| Slide 10 (Results) | Proof — weave throughout | Throughout |
| Slide 11 (Qualification) | Pre-close framing | ~38:00 |
| Slide 12 (Investment) | State the price | ~38:00 |
| Slide 13 (Guarantee) | Handle objections | 38:00–48:00 |
| Slide 14 (CTA) | The Close | 48:00–55:00 |

### Key Transitions (What to Say Between Slides)

**Slide 2 → 3:** *"But here's the thing — there's a reason we're different from every other Amazon program out there. AI is changing everything."*

**Slide 3 → 4:** *"Let me walk you through exactly how the system works."*

**Slide 4 → 5:** *"The system runs on three pillars. Let me break each one down."*

**Slide 8 → 9:** *"And there's one more thing most people don't expect..."*

**Slide 9 → 10:** *"Don't just take my word for it. Let me show you what our students are doing."*

**Slide 10 → 11:** *"Now — I want to be straight with you. This isn't for everyone."*

**Slide 11 → 12:** *"If you're in that first column, here's what the investment looks like."*

**Slide 12 → 13:** *"And we back this with a guarantee."*

**Slide 13 → 14:** *"So — based on everything we've talked about — does this feel like the right fit?"*
