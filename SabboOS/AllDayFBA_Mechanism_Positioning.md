## MECHANISM NAME OPTIONS

### Option 1: "AI Amazon Scaling" (RECOMMENDED)
- **One-liner:** "I help people build $10K-$50K/month Amazon businesses using AI-powered product research and brand partnerships."
- **Why it works:** Simple. Three power words (AI + Amazon + Scaling). Everyone knows what each word means. Together they sound new.
- **Urgency angle:** "AI is changing Amazon selling RIGHT NOW. The sellers who adopt AI first will dominate. The ones who don't will get left behind."
- **Content title test:** "How AI Amazon Scaling is replacing traditional FBA" — would you click? Yes.

### Option 2: "AI Seller OS"
- **One-liner:** "I built an operating system that uses AI to find, source, and scale Amazon products — and I teach others how to install it in their business."
- **Why it works:** "OS" = system, not course. Feels professional, tech-forward, modern.
- **Urgency angle:** "The old way of doing Amazon is dead. AI Seller OS is the new standard."
- **Downside:** Might be too tech-jargon for beginners.

### Option 3: "The AI FBA Method"
- **One-liner:** "The AI FBA Method is a step-by-step system that uses artificial intelligence to find profitable Amazon products and scale to $10K/month in 90 days."
- **Why it works:** "Method" = proprietary, proven, specific. Easy to title videos around.
- **Urgency angle:** "The AI FBA Method is why I went from my mom's basement to 7 figures."
- **Content title test:** "I tried The AI FBA Method for 30 days — here's what happened" — strong.

### Option 4: "AI Brand Scaling for Amazon"
- **One-liner:** "I show people how to use AI to partner with real brands and scale them on Amazon — no private label, no guessing, no competing on price."
- **Why it works:** Combines your two unique angles (AI + brand partnerships).
- **Downside:** A bit long as a brand name. Better as a description than a name.

### Option 5: "Amazon AI Arbitrage"
- **One-liner:** "Amazon AI Arbitrage is using artificial intelligence to find price gaps between suppliers and Amazon — automatically."
- **Why it works:** Ben Bader's suggestion. Catchy. Creates FOMO.
- **Downside:** "Arbitrage" might sound risky or confusing to beginners.

Either way, you become **the AI Amazon guy** — which is currently unclaimed territory.
