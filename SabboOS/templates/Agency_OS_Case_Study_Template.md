# Home Services Company -- From Unpredictable Referrals to $285K/Mo Predictable Revenue in 6 Months

---

## Client Snapshot

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Industry** | Home Services (HVAC + Plumbing) |
| **Annual Revenue** | $1.4M (at engagement start) |
| **Team Size** | 14 people |
| **Location** | Dallas-Fort Worth, TX |
| **Engagement Tier** | Growth ($10,000/mo) |
| **Duration** | 6 months (ongoing) |
| **Channels** | Meta Ads, Google Ads, Email |

---

## The Situation

When this mid-market home services company came to us, they were:

- **Stuck at $120K/mo with no visibility into what was driving revenue** -- They were spending $15K/mo on Meta and Google with no attribution. Good months and bad months happened randomly and nobody knew why.

- **Cycling through agencies with nothing to show for it** -- Three agencies in two years. Each one promised "more leads" and delivered dashboards full of impressions. The founder was still the de facto marketing director.

- **Losing deals to follow-up gaps** -- Leads came in, but there was no automated nurture. The office manager called leads manually when she had time. Response time averaged 6+ hours. Competitors were closing the same leads faster.

- **Referral-dependent with no acquisition engine** -- 70% of revenue came from referrals and repeat customers. Growth was capped by the founder's personal network.

**In their words:**
> "I was spending $15K a month and couldn't tell you if it was working. My last agency sent me a 30-page report every month that I never read because it was all clicks and impressions. I needed someone to actually fix the machine, not just report on it."

---

## The Diagnosis

After our strategy session, we identified **the absence of post-click infrastructure** as the core bottleneck holding back growth.

**What we found:**
- No attribution tracking. $15K/mo in ad spend with no conversion tracking, no UTMs, no way to tie a closed deal back to a specific campaign.
- Email list of 8,200 past customers with zero automated sequences. Every lead was followed up by one person making manual phone calls during business hours.
- Landing page converting at 1.4%. No VSL, no social proof, no application form -- just a generic contact form buried below the fold.

**Impact on revenue:**
We estimated this was leaving $50K-$80K/mo on the table in unconverted leads, lost repeat business, and referral revenue they never asked for.

---

## What We Built

### Week 1-2: Discovery + Infrastructure Foundation
- Full baseline audit: mapped the entire customer journey from ad click to closed job
- Meta Pixel and GA4 installed from scratch; UTM taxonomy built for every campaign
- CRM pipeline restructured from "New Lead / In Progress / Closed" to 7 stages with automated triggers
- Analyzed top 5 local competitors via Meta Ad Library and Google Ads Transparency

### Week 3-4: Asset Production + Launch
- Landing page rebuilt with video testimonial, before/after project photos, and a 5-question qualification form -- conversion rate jumped from 1.4% to 6.1%
- 5-email lead intake sequence + 3-email no-show recovery built and automated in GHL
- 3 ad angles launched: emergency/urgency ("AC out? Same-day service"), trust/proof ("1,200+ DFW homes served"), and seasonal ("Pre-summer tune-up: $49 inspection")
- Attribution dashboard live in Looker Studio -- every lead tracked from ad click to booked job

### Month 2: Optimization + Scaling
- Killed the seasonal angle (high CPL, low close rate), doubled spend on emergency/urgency angle
- A/B tested landing page headline -- "Same-Day HVAC Service in DFW" beat "Professional HVAC Services" by 41%
- Added retargeting for LP visitors who did not submit the form -- CPL on retargeting was 60% lower than cold
- Automated post-job review request email -- generated 34 new Google reviews in 30 days

### Month 3-6: Compounding Results
- Scaled daily ad spend from $200 to $500 while maintaining $38 CPL (below $45 target)
- Launched Google Search campaigns for "emergency HVAC [city]" keywords -- highest-intent leads at $22 CPL
- Win-back email sequence to past customers generated 89 re-bookings in 90 days (zero ad spend)
- Referral program automated: post-job email offers $50 credit for referrals -- 23% of new customers now come from referrals triggered by the system

---

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After (6 Months) | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly Revenue** | $120,000 | $285,000 | +137% |
| **Cost Per Lead** | $94 | $38 | -60% |
| **Lead Volume** | 18/mo (inconsistent) | 92/mo (predictable) | +411% |
| **Close Rate** | 12% | 28% | +16pp |
| **Show Rate** | 54% | 78% | +24pp |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost** | $1,200 | $340 | -72% |
| **ROAS (Blended)** | 1.1x | 4.8x | +3.7x |
| **Email Revenue %** | 0% | 24% | +24pp |
| **ROI on Retainer** | -- | 16.5x | -- |

**Headline stat:** Revenue increased 137% in 6 months -- from $120K to $285K/mo -- while customer acquisition cost dropped 72%, all without a single internal marketing hire.

---

## Client Quote

> "For the first time in 14 years of business, I know exactly where every lead comes from and what it costs to close them. We went from $120K to $285K a month and I didn't hire a single person on the marketing side. The system just runs."
>
> -- **Representative Client**, Owner, DFW Home Services Company

---

## Key Takeaway

The bottleneck was never ad spend -- it was the absence of infrastructure behind the ads. Once we installed tracking, nurture sequences, a real landing page, and a post-job referral system, the same ad budget produced 4x the results. The lesson: systems compound, campaigns don't.
