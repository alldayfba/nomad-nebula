### 4. 30-Day Install Timeline

We do not spend 90 days "getting set up." Your growth operating system goes live in 30 days.

| Week | Phase | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 1** | Kickoff + Discovery | 90-min kickoff call. Access provisioning (ad accounts, CRM, analytics, website). Brand voice extraction. Competitor audit. ICP deep-dive. Baseline metrics audit (current CAC, LTV, conversion rates). 90-day OKRs and KPI scorecard set. |
| **Week 2** | Infrastructure Build | Tracking and attribution installed (pixel, GA4, UTMs). Funnel architecture finalized. CRM and email flows built. Landing page built or rebuilt. Ad account structured (campaigns, ad sets, creative slots). |
| **Week 3** | Creative + Content | Ad creative production (3 angles: pain, aspiration, mechanism). Landing page copy finalized. Email sequences loaded and tested. Social content calendar drafted. Retargeting creative set produced. |
| **Week 4** | Launch + Calibrate | Campaigns go live. Daily monitoring begins. First data review at day 3. Initial optimizations applied. First weekly performance call. Baseline CPL and CPQL established. |

**By day 30:** Campaigns are live, data is flowing, and we have a clear picture of what is working. No more guessing.

### 5. Ongoing Operating Rhythm (Month 2+)

Once the system is installed, we operate it like clockwork.

**Daily:**
- Campaign monitoring — spend pacing, CTR, CPL, conversion tracking
- Bid and budget adjustments based on real-time performance
- Lead response monitoring (if applicable)
- Creative fatigue detection and rotation triggers

**Weekly:**
- 30-min performance call with your growth operator
- Dashboard update with key metrics and trends
- A/B test results review (headlines, creative, landing page elements)
- Next-week action items documented and assigned

**Monthly:**
- Full strategy review — what worked, what didn't, what changes
- Budget reallocation based on channel performance
- New creative production cycle (fresh hooks, angles, formats)
- 30-day growth plan with specific targets and initiatives

**Quarterly:**
- 90-min business review with executive team
- OKR reset and goal recalibration
- Offer and positioning review
- Scaling roadmap for next quarter
