# Amazon OS — Webinar Script v2 (Full Production Draft)
> **Runtime:** ~90 minutes (~13,500 words at 150 wpm)
> **Format:** Live webinar, slides + talking head + screen share
> **Framework:** Jason Fladlien "Genius Webinars" 4-Part Structure
> **Audience:** Beginners, plateaued sellers, investors
> **Tone:** Direct, operator-level, anti-grifter, authentic
> **Brand:** 24/7 Profits — AI Amazon Scaling

---

## Framework Map

| Fladlien 4-Part | Section | Timing | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Part 1: Introduction** | Welcome, Authority, Proof, Commitment, Pre-Handle, Hope, Intrigue | 0:00–15:00 | Build trust, pre-handle objections, set the frame |
| **Part 2: Content** | Problem, The Shift, 5-Step System, AI Demo, Student Walkthroughs, 3 Pillars | 15:00–65:00 | Teach real value, demonstrate the mechanism, stack proof |
| **Part 3: Transition** | Recap, Yes Momentum, Two Choices | 65:00–70:00 | Bridge from content to offer with agreement escalation |
| **Part 4: Close** | Offer Stack, Bonuses, Guarantee, Qualification, Objections, CTA | 70:00–90:00 | Present the offer, handle resistance, drive action |
