### On the Call — Hit These 3 Points
1. **Uncover the real problem** (not the surface symptom)
2. **Connect the problem to consequences** (cost of inaction)
3. **Position the Inner Circle as the bridge** (from where they are to where they want to be)

### The Pre-Frame Question (Minute 10–15)
> "What's actually prevented you from taking that first step until now?"

Whatever they answer IS the objection they'll throw at the close. Dissolve it during discovery.

### Investment Presentation
> "The investment is $5,000. Normally $10,000 — we dropped it for 2026 because we're growing the community. That pricing isn't permanent."

State it directly. Don't apologize. Don't over-explain. Let it sit.

### The Close
> "Based on everything we talked about — does this feel like the right move for you?"

Then: *"Why do you feel that way?"* — Let them sell themselves.
