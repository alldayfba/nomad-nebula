Weeks 2-4 — Infrastructure Install Days 8-30

Week 2: Tracking + Attribution

  • Install/verify Meta Pixel on all pages
  • Install/verify GA4 events + conversions
  • Set up UTM taxonomy
  • Configure attribution dashboard
  • Audit existing funnel
  • Finalize funnel architecture
  • Configure CRM pipeline stages

Week 3: Email Sequences + Ad Creative + Landing Page

  • Lead intake email sequence (5 emails)
  • Pre-call nurture sequence (2 emails)
  • No-show re-engagement (3 emails)
  • Long-term nurture (weekly template)
  • Win-back sequence (30/60/90 day)
  • Ad creative briefs (3 angles)
  • Ad copy: 5 cold traffic + 3 retargeting scripts
  • Landing page copy + design/build

Week 4: Campaign Build + Launch

  • Ad account structure (campaigns, ad sets)
  • Creative uploaded and configured
  • Audience segments built (cold, warm, retarget)
  • Landing page tested (mobile + desktop, all forms)
  • Email sequences loaded + tested
  • Tracking end-to-end verified
  • Client review call (pre-launch approval)
  • Campaigns go live
