Morning
Objection training: Study all 5 categories from Battle Cards. Listen to calls where objections were handled well.
Afternoon
Black Marker (Stages 5-7). Role-play Problem Awareness + Pre-Frame. Practice the Revealing Question.

Thursday

Morning
Product knowledge deep-dive. Walk through the Growth OS, 30-day install, operating rhythm, data layer. Walk through pitch deck.
Afternoon
Black Marker (Stages 7-8). Role-play Pitch + Price stages. Practice stating price and sitting in silence.

Morning
Full mock strategy call with manager. Manager plays a skeptical $3M founder burned by 3 agencies. Get feedback.
