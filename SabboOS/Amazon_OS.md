# Amazon OS — Amazon Coaching Program
> Last Updated: 2026-02-20 | Version 1.0

---

## 1. OFFER POSITIONING

**Category:** High-ticket Amazon FBA coaching program (Done-With-You)

**Target Student:**
- Tier A: Complete beginner with $5K–$20K+ to invest, motivated by financial freedom / income replacement
- Tier B: Existing Amazon seller doing $0–$50K/mo who's plateaued and wants to scale
- Tier C: Business owner or investor who wants to add Amazon as a cash-flowing asset

**Avoid:**
- People with no capital (under $3K)
- People looking for passive income with zero effort
- Students who've already made significant money on Amazon (wrong positioning)

**Positioning Statement:**
Not a course. Not a community. A coaching system that gets you to your first (or next) $10K month on Amazon — with an operator beside you the whole way.

**Market Tier:** High-ticket coaching ($3K–$10K program, payment plans available)

---

## 2. CORE PROMISE

> "We'll take you from zero (or stuck) to a profitable, running Amazon FBA business in 90 days — or we keep working with you until you do."

**Primary Outcome:** Launch a profitable Amazon product with $5K+/mo in revenue within 90 days.

**Secondary Outcomes:**
- Know exactly how to research, source, and launch products that win
- Have a repeatable system for scaling beyond the first product
- Never be dependent on a course or guru — own the skillset permanently

**Guarantee Structure (Optional):**
- "Work with us until you hit $X in revenue" (conditional on effort benchmarks)
- OR 30-day satisfaction refund if no work has begun

## 6. AD STRATEGY

### Platform Priority
1. **Meta (Facebook/Instagram)** — Broadest reach, best for lead magnet funnels
2. **YouTube** — High-intent traffic for "make money on Amazon" content
3. **TikTok** — Lower CPL for younger demographic (Tier A students)
4. **Google Search** — Intent capture ("Amazon FBA coaching," "learn Amazon FBA")

### Creative Framework

**3 Core Angles:**

**Angle 1 — Income Replacement / Freedom**
Hook: "How [Name] quit her 9-5 in 4 months selling on Amazon."
Format: Student story, UGC-style, 30–60s
CTA: "Get the free training that started it all"

**Angle 2 — Skepticism Breaker**
Hook: "Yes, Amazon FBA still works in 2026. Here's the proof."
Format: Talking head with data/screenshots, 45–90s
CTA: "Watch the breakdown → [link]"

**Angle 3 — The Mechanism**
Hook: "Most people fail on Amazon because they skip step 2."
Format: Educational hook, fast-paced, 30–60s
CTA: "I'll walk you through all 5 steps for free"

### Budget Structure
- Testing phase: $75–150/day (split across 3 angles)
- Scale: 2–3x budget on winning angle after 3–5 days of data
- Retargeting: 20% of total budget
- YouTube: $20–50/day as a parallel test

### KPIs
- Cost per Lead (opt-in): Target < $8–15
- Cost per Application: Target < $75–150
- Cost per Booked Call: Target < $200
- Cost per Enrolled Student: Target < $800–1,500 (vs. $3K–$10K revenue)

## 7. LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE

### Version A: Lead Magnet LP (Volume Funnel)

**Above the Fold:**
- Headline: Outcome-first (e.g., "Discover the 5-Step System to Launch a Profitable Amazon Product in 90 Days")
- Subheadline: Who it's for + credibility hint
- Opt-in form: First name + email
- CTA button: "Get Free Access"
- No navigation, no distractions

**Below the Fold:**
- What they'll learn (3–5 bullets)
- Social proof bar (students enrolled, revenue generated, etc.)
- Short bio / credibility blurb
- Repeat CTA

---

### Version B: VSL Application LP (High-Ticket Direct)

**Above the Fold:**
- Headline: Bold promise
- VSL embed (autoplay, sound off, captions on)
- CTA below video: "Apply for a Free Strategy Call"

**Section 2 — The Problem**
- The broken path most FBA beginners take
- Agitate: wasted money, wrong products, black hat risks, algorithm changes

**Section 3 — The System**
- Introduce the coaching framework
- Name the methodology (e.g., "The 5-Phase Amazon Operator System")
- Visual breakdown of each phase

**Section 4 — Results**
- 2–3 case studies with real metrics
- Revenue screenshots, product ranking proof

**Section 5 — Who This Is For**
- Specific qualifying criteria
- Who it is NOT for (repels bad fits)

**Section 6 — Application CTA**
- 5–7 question application form
- "We only work with X students per cohort"
- Rebook page on submission

## 8. VSL OUTLINE

**Total Runtime:** 15–20 minutes

### Hook (0:00–1:30)
- Counterintuitive claim or pattern interrupt
- "Amazon FBA is not dead — but the way 95% of people are doing it is."
- Preview what they're about to learn

### Credibility & Story (1:30–4:00)
- Your story (brief — not a biography)
- When you started, the mistakes you made, when it clicked
- Current proof: your store, student results
- Why you're qualified to teach this

### Problem Breakdown (4:00–7:00)
- The 3–5 most common reasons people fail on Amazon
- Be specific: wrong product selection, no PPC strategy, no ranking plan, copying saturated markets
- Make them feel understood

### The System (7:00–12:00)
- Introduce your framework by name
- Walk through each phase:
  1. Product Research (the filter system)
  2. Supplier Selection & Negotiation
  3. Listing Creation (SEO + conversion)
  4. Launch Sequence (ranking strategy)
  5. PPC Setup + Scaling
- Keep it conceptual enough to show value, detailed enough to be credible

### Student Results (12:00–15:00)
- 2–3 student stories in before/after format
- Real numbers: investment, timeline, monthly revenue
- Diverse profiles (beginner, someone who failed before, side hustler)

### The Offer (15:00–17:00)
- What's included in the coaching program
- What makes it different from a course
- Investment (or "apply to find out")
- The guarantee

### CTA + Close (17:00–End)
- Apply for a strategy call
- Explain what happens on the call (no pressure, just clarity)
- Urgency: cohort model, limited spots, or price increase
- Final restate of the promise

| # | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediately after booking | "You're booked — here's how to prepare" | Set expectations, increase show rate |

| # | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|

- Format: 300–500 word insight email

- CTA: Soft — "If you're ready to take this seriously, reply 'ready'"
- Cadence: 1x per week, indefinitely

| # | Timing | Angle |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 30 days | New student result + "the window is still open" |

### Retargeting Creative Rotation
- Week 1: Case study / result focus
- Week 2: Objection handler ("Is Amazon too saturated?")
- Week 3: Urgency / cohort close
- Refresh creative every 10–14 days

### Retargeting Frequency
- Cap: 2–3 impressions/day per person
- Duration: 30–45 days max before suppressing

## 11. FULFILLMENT WORKFLOW

### Onboarding (Days 1–3)
- [ ] Welcome email + portal access sent
- [ ] Onboarding questionnaire (goals, capital, timeline, prior experience)
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled (60 min)
- [ ] Added to community / group
- [ ] Milestone tracker shared

### Kickoff Call (Day 1–5)
- [ ] Define 90-day goal (specific revenue target, product category)
- [ ] Capital audit (how much to invest in inventory)
- [ ] Account setup checklist provided
- [ ] Week 1 action items assigned

### Core Curriculum (Weeks 1–12)

**Module 1 — Foundation (Week 1–2)**
- Amazon account setup and compliance
- Seller Central navigation
- Business entity + finances setup

**Module 2 — Product Research (Week 2–4)**
- Research methodology and filters
- Tools: Helium 10 / Jungle Scout workflow
- Validating demand and competition
- Live product research session (coaching call)

**Module 3 — Sourcing (Week 4–6)**
- Alibaba / 1688 supplier outreach templates
- Negotiation framework
- Sampling process
- Quality control checklist

**Module 4 — Listing Creation (Week 6–7)**
- Keyword research (main and long-tail)
- Title, bullet points, description (conversion-optimized)
- A+ content structure
- Photography brief

**Module 5 — Launch Strategy (Week 7–9)**
- Honeymoon period strategy
- Review request system (compliant)
- Launch sequence (PPC + external traffic)
- Ranking velocity framework

**Module 6 — PPC Mastery (Week 9–11)**
- Auto → Manual campaign structure
- Bidding strategy
- ACoS targets and break-even math
- Optimization cadence

**Module 7 — Scaling (Week 11–12)**
- Second product criteria
- Inventory forecasting
- Brand Registry and A+ content
- Wholesale / bundling opportunities

### Coaching Cadence
- **Week 1–4:** 2x per week group calls (1 hour each)
- **Week 5–8:** 1x per week group call + 1x biweekly 1:1 (30 min)
- **Week 9–12:** 1x per week group call + async Loom feedback on listings/PPC

### Support Infrastructure
- Async Q&A: Private community or Slack (48-hr response SLA)
- Emergency: Direct message for account issues
- Recordings: All calls recorded and uploaded within 24 hrs

### Milestone Tracking

| Milestone | Target Date | Check-in |
|---|---|---|
| Account live | Day 7 | Week 1 call |
| Product selected | Day 21 | Week 3 call |
| Supplier confirmed | Day 35 | Week 5 call |
| Listing live | Day 55 | Week 8 call |
| First sale | Day 60–70 | Week 9 call |
| First $1K revenue month | Day 70–90 | Week 12 review |

### Retention / Upsell
- At 90 days: Offer continuation coaching (monthly retainer, $500–1,500/mo)
- Or: Mastermind / advanced group for students at $10K+/mo
- Referral program: 10% commission for enrolled referrals
