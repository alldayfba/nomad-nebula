```
1 YouTube Long-Form Video

    ├── 2-3 TikTok videos (reformat clips)
    ├── 1 YouTube Short (strongest hook)
    ├── 1 Instagram Carousel (key takeaways as slides)
    ├── 1 Email newsletter (summary + CTA)
    └── 3-5 Instagram Stories (behind the scenes of filming)
```
