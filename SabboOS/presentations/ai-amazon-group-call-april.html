Deal Score

Demand — Is It Selling?

BSR = Best Sellers Rank. Lower number = more popular.

40

BSR under 500

Top seller — flies off shelves

35

BSR 500-2,000

Strong seller — consistent daily sales

20

BSR 5,000-20,000

Decent — sells regularly

2

BSR over 250,000

Barely moving — avoid

Deal Score

Competition + Price

Competition (25 pts)

1-2 FBA sellers = 25 pts (great!)

3-5 sellers = 20 pts (still good)

Amazon on listing = 5 pts (avoid)

Price Sweet Spot (20 pts)

$15-$50 = 20 pts (ideal FBA range)

$50-$100 = 15 pts (higher risk)

Under $8 = 5 pts (fees kill you)

If Amazon is selling the product — move on. They almost always win the Buy Box.
