Today's Call

SDR Onboarding Call Agenda

March 26, 2026 — Follow this checklist during the call. Click sections to expand.

Total call time 45–60 min
1
Welcome & Set the Tone
Energy, expectations, what this is
3 min
  • Welcome everyone — names, where they're from
  • Set the tone: "This is a performance environment. If you execute, you'll make great money. If you don't, we'll part ways fast. No hard feelings."
  • Explain the call structure: "I'm going to walk you through everything — who we are, what we sell, the script, how you get paid, and then you're dialing today."
Talk Track
"Welcome to 247 Growth. I'm Sabbo. Here's the deal — I brought you on because I think you can produce. Today I'm going to give you everything you need to start booking calls. By end of day, I expect dials and bookings. Let's get into it."
2
Who We Are & What We Do
The company, the offer, why clients pay us
5 min
  • "We're a growth agency. We build and operate full-stack growth systems for businesses."
  • Explain the 3 services: Websites (high-converting, not template garbage), Ads (Meta/Google, managed), AI Automation (follow-ups, CRM, booking)
  • "We're not like other agencies — we're operators, not account managers. Senior people embedded in the business."
  • "Clients pay $5K–$25K/month. Most deals you'll set will be in the $3K–$10K range."
  • Share screen → walk through Slides 2–3 of the onboarding slideshow
Don't go deep here. They don't need to be experts on the offer — just enough to sound credible and find pain.
3
What We're Selling Right Now
Websites + Ads — the current pitch angles
5 min
  • "Right now we're going after real estate agents."
  • "Most of these people have no website, or a trash template one. They're posting on Instagram but not converting."
  • Explain the Website angle: "Hey, I tried to find your listings online but couldn't. You're losing customers because they can't find you."
  • Explain the Ads angle: "You're relying on referrals. What if you had a system bringing in new leads every single day?"
  • "You don't need to be an expert. Find the pain, book the call. The closer handles the rest."
  • Share screen → Slide 3 (What You're Pitching Right Now)
4
Your Role — What You Do and Don't Do
Filter, not pitcher. Book, don't close.
3 min
  • "Your ONLY job: get qualified founders on a strategy call."
  • "You do NOT pitch the offer. You do NOT talk pricing. You do NOT try to close."
  • "You find pain, confirm fit, and book the call. That's it."
  • "Say 'strategy session' — NEVER 'sales call' or 'pitch.'"
  • "3 qualified bookings beats 8 unqualified. Close rate on bad leads is 0%."
  • Share screen → Slide 4 (Your Role) + Slide 5 (ICP — Who to Book vs Drop)
5
The Script — 4 Openers + 6-Phase Flow
Walk through each phase with examples
12 min
  • Explain: "You have 4 opener styles. Pick the one that fits the lead."
  • Style 1 — Customer Approach: "I was trying to find a listing but couldn't on your site..." (walk through the full script)
  • Style 2 — Familiarity Play: "[Name]? What's up! We spoke a while back about getting you more clients..." (walk through response handling)
  • Style 3 — Website Gap: "I tried to schedule with you but there was no website..." (walk through)
  • Style 4 — Direct Cold Open: Classic "do you have 30 seconds?" (walk through)
  • Walk through Phase 2: The Hook — "Is your growth systematic or figuring it out?"
  • Walk through Phase 3: Dig Into Pain — the 5 questions, then recap
  • Walk through Phase 4: The Bridge — connect pain to the strategy call
  • Walk through Phase 5: Book the Call — either/or times + tie-downs (decision maker + financial)
  • Walk through Phase 6: Show Rate System — 6 touchpoints
  • Share screen → Slides 7–11 (full script walkthrough)
This is the longest section. Go slow. Read the scripts out loud. Make them repeat key lines back to you. "What do you say when they ask what you do?" — drill it.
Key Point to Hammer
"Follow the script. Don't freelance. Don't 'make it your own' in week 1. Earn that right. The script works — trust it."
6
Objection Handling
Top 5 pushbacks and exactly what to say
5 min
  • "I'm not interested" → "Is that because growth is dialed in, or timing?"
  • "Just send me an email" → "What's best email? And what's the biggest challenge right now?"
  • "We already have an agency" → "Are you happy with the results?"
  • "What do you guys do?" → Short answer + bridge back to booking
  • "I don't have time" → "That's exactly why — 30 min to get your time back"
  • Share screen → Slide 13 (Objection Handling)
Principle
"Handle and redirect. Don't argue. Don't debate. Acknowledge what they said, redirect to the pain, move forward. If they say no twice — respect it, move on. Next dial."
7
How You Get Paid
$20/show + 7% cash collected
3 min
  • $20 per shown call. "Not per booking — per SHOW. This is why the 6-touchpoint system matters."
  • 7% of cash collected. "When your lead closes and pays, you get 7% of every dollar. $5K deal = $350."
  • Walk through the math: "2 bookings/day × 80% show = 8 shows/week = $160. Plus 2 closes at $5K = $700. That's $860/week."
  • Growth path: Setter → Senior Setter → Team Lead → Closer
  • Share screen → Slide 19 (Compensation)
8
KPIs, Schedule & Expectations
The standard. Perform or we part ways.
5 min
  • 100+ dials per day. Not 50. One hundred. Minimum.
  • 2-4 bookings per day. Quality > quantity.
  • 80%+ show rate. If they don't show, that's on YOU — follow up harder.
  • EOD report every day. Dials, connects, bookings, shows. Miss one = conversation with me.
  • Walk through the daily schedule: 9 AM pipeline → 10 AM dial block 1 → 12 PM lunch → 1 PM dial block 2 → 4 PM log + report
  • "If you can't get through your list today, this isn't the right fit."
  • "Follow the script. Don't freelance. Earn that right."
  • Share screen → Slides 15–18 (Schedule, KPIs, Math, Expectations)
Close This Section With
"I'm not saying this to scare you. I'm saying this because the people who take this seriously make real money here. The people who treat it like a side gig don't last. I want you to last."
9
Today's Setup — Get Them Dialing
Hand out leads, set up tools, start calling
5 min
  • Send each setter their personal lead CSV (setter-1 through setter-5)
  • "Open it in Google Sheets. Each row is a lead — name, phone, website, address, Google Maps link."
  • "Add columns for: Call Outcome, Notes, Follow-Up Date, Booked Y/N"
  • "GHL is down right now — so log everything in the sheet. When CRM is back, we migrate."
  • Send links: Offer Breakdown (study guide) + Onboarding Slideshow (reference)
  • "You have ~65 leads. Do 60-second research on each one before you dial. If you can't get through them all today, we have a problem."
  • Set up voice chat (Discord/Zoom) — "Everyone dials in the same room. I'll be listening."
  • "Questions? Then let's start dialing."

10
Q&A + Close
Answer questions, set expectations, go
5 min
  • "Any questions before we start?"
  • Answer questions — keep it tight, don't let it spiral
  • "Alright. You have your leads, you have the script, you know the expectations. Let's go book some calls."
  • "EOD report to me by 4:30 PM. Dials, connects, bookings. No exceptions."
  • "Let's make money."
Final Energy Check
"I believe in each of you — that's why you're here. Now prove me right. First person to book a call drops it in the group chat. Let's go."
