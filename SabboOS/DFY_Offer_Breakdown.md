## Objection Handling

**"Why not just do the coaching program?"**
> "The coaching program is incredible and most people should start there. The DFY is for people who have the capital but not the time — or who want the absolute best operators running their business from day one. It's the same system, just we're the ones executing."

**"$20K is a lot."**
> "Half of that doesn't come due until we've made you $100K. We're literally putting our money where our mouth is. If we don't perform, you save $10K."

**"What if it doesn't work?"**
> "We don't stop until you hit $100K. That's the guarantee. And half our fee is tied to your results — we're more motivated than anyone to make this work."

**"What's the 10% for?"**
> "After $100K, you have a choice: keep us running everything at 10% of profits, or take over yourself. By that point you'll have the knowledge from coaching to do either. It's 100% your call."

**"Can I just do it myself after?"**
> "That's the whole point. You learn everything through coaching while we operate. At $100K, you can take the wheel with full confidence."
