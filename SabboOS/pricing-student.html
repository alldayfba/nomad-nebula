FAQ

I'm in Inner Circle. Will my unlimited credits ever go away?

No. If you PIF'd Inner Circle at any enrollment date, unlimited is yours forever. That was the promise and it stays the promise — we'll never revoke it. The only cap we enforce on you is the 25 tracked-seller limit, which exists so one user can't slow Keepa down for the whole community.

Why is there a tracked-seller cap even on unlimited plans?

Every tracked seller gets its catalog re-scanned on a schedule. Those scans cost us Keepa API tokens. If one user tracked 500 sellers, they'd burn the shared token pool and every scan everyone else runs would slow down. The 25 cap is generous — no serious arbitrage sourcer actually needs more. If you do, book a call and we'll whitelist you.

I'm on Accelerator. What happens when I run out of credits mid-month?

Two options. (1) Buy a top-up pack — starts at $19 for 500 credits, they never expire. (2) Wait until the 1st of next month when your 2,000 refill hits. Your existing tool access doesn't disappear — you just can't run new AI actions until credits return.

If I upgrade from Accelerator to Inner Circle, do I get a discount for what I already paid?

Yes. Whatever you've paid toward Accelerator is credited 100% toward your Inner Circle balance. So $2K PIF → $8K to step up. Talk to your coach to lock the rate before it changes.

My payment plan card is about to expire. What happens?

Update it in Settings → Payment Methods before the next charge date. If the charge fails, we auto-retry at day 1, 3, 7 with email notifications. On day 10 your tools pause — not your access to content or Discord, just the AI tools — until the card is fixed.

Can I cancel anytime?

SaaS subscriptions: yes, self-service in Settings, cancels at end of current billing cycle (you keep access through the paid period). Coaching programs: see your enrollment agreement — generally 14-day refund window before onboarding, then the program runs its full term. Payment plans are commitments.

Where does my money actually go?

Real talk: Keepa API tokens (~$0.10 per seller scan), scraper compute, Whop/Fanbasis processing (~3%), coaching calls (my time), course platform hosting, and the Claude Max plan ($200/mo flat) that powers Nova Chat for the whole community. AI features feel free because the Max plan is a fixed cost, not per-token. That's why we can give you unlimited Nova without going broke.
