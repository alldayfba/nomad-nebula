## 2. Core Philosophy

### Offers First, Ads Second
"If the offer is really sexy, the landing page doesn't need to be that insane. The ad and the offer sells itself." Every underperforming campaign Wojo audits has the same root cause: weak offer, not weak ads.

### Omnipresence > Single Channel
Don't put all budget on one platform. Broadcast across Facebook, Google, TikTok simultaneously so the prospect keeps seeing the brand everywhere. The goal is to flood the prospect's data pipeline from multiple angles.

### Anti-AI on Copy
"If you can't write verbiage, you're not a marketer." AI should be used for personalization or templating proven copy — never for generating original creative. Human psychology, copywriting, and self-awareness are the three essential media buyer skills.

### Break-Even Front, Profit Back
Run a self-liquidating offer on the front end so ad spend partially or fully pays for itself. The backend (high-ticket coaching, consulting, agency retainer) is where real profit lives. At scale: $12K/day spend, $11K return on front end, profit on backend conversions.

## 3. Frameworks

### 3.1 — The 4 Pillars Framework (Core Methodology)

Every client intervention runs through these four pillars:

**Pillar 1: Irresistible Offer**
- Simple solve — one clear problem solved
- Quick win — implementable in 15 minutes
- Unique framing — feels like it can't be Googled
- Urgency built in naturally, not by fake scarcity

**Pillar 2: High-Converting Landing Page**
- Right messaging + compelling copy + social proof
- Fast load speed
- Prominent support numbers
- Clear product instructions
- One-page sales format broadcasting the irresistible offer

**Pillar 3: Omnipresent Ad Targeting**
- Ads across Facebook, Google, TikTok simultaneously
- Prospect sees brand from multiple angles
- Creates perception of brand scale and authority

**Pillar 4: Data-Driven KPI Tracking**
- Predictability and profitability are key
- Establish clear KPIs based on industry benchmarks
- Never scale without understanding unit economics
- Test with confidence, optimize and scale what works

### 3.3 — Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) Funnel

The primary framework for scaling cold traffic profitably.

**Funnel structure:**
```
Ad → Low-ticket ($7-$37) → Order bump ($17-$47) → Upsell ($37-$197) → Calendar booking
```

**Target economics:**
- 1.1-1.2x ROAS on the front end (acceptable — not trying to profit here)
- Replenish ad spend at zero net cost
- Generate qualified buyers, not freebie-seekers
- Buyers have 3-5x higher show rates and close rates than free lead magnet leads

**Wojo's own SLO:**
- Sold 100 ad templates for $17
- Ran $3-4K/day in spend
- Profitable on front end while filling agency pipeline with buyers

**Current scale:**
- $12K/day spend
- $11K/day front-end return
- Backend profit from high-ticket conversions ($3.5K-$30K)

### 3.4 — Scaling Methodology

**Vertical scaling:** Increase budget on same campaign. Rule: 20% increase every 3-5 days. Slower but stable.

**Horizontal scaling:** Duplicate winning ad sets into new audiences or campaigns. Faster growth but requires proven creative first.

**Preference:** Both simultaneously. Vertical on proven winners while horizontal testing runs.

**Break-even front-end is acceptable at scale.** The ad spend is lead gen infrastructure, not a standalone profit center.

### 3.5 — Ad Creative Philosophy

**Hook is everything.** First 3 seconds determines performance.

**The hook, the angle, or the offer — one of these three must be outstanding for an ad to work.** You don't need all three to be perfect. One outstanding element is enough.

**Ad structure template (4 parts):**
1. **Callout:** "Attention Amazon sellers who are stuck under $10K/mo..."
2. **Offer sentence:** [Who] + [Result] + [Risk reversal]
3. **Proof/authority:** "After helping 200+ people get their first profitable product..."
4. **CTA:** "Click below to book a free strategy call"

**Volume:** High creative output per account. The creative IS the targeting.
