## Webinar Show-Up Rate Optimization Framework

### The Context
- This system is for **cold paid traffic → weekly webinars** (not warm organic audiences)
- Organic audiences can get 50% show rates even with bad emails — this is for the harder cold traffic game

### 9-Component System

**1. SMS Reminders (Immediate Post-Registration)**
- Fire immediately after registration
- Question-based format (not generic reminders)
- Redirect back to thank you page

**2. Email Flows (2-3x/day)**
- Value-based, not just reminders
- Go into unique mechanisms — explain the "why" behind showing up
- Answer common questions
- Frame webinar as "the most important thing to show up to this week"
- All emails reroute back to the thank you page

**3. Thank You Page as Conversion Hub**
- FAQs section
- All testimonials displayed prominently
- Testimonials are **interview-style videos** (NOT selfie testimonials)
- One testimonial per "type" of ideal prospect → each viewer sees someone like them
- Goal: conviction through relatability and resonance

**4. VIP Upsells (Pre-Webinar)**
- Push 5-10% of registrants to a VIP offer
- VIP buyers automatically have higher show rates (credit card already out)
- VIP includes implementation call → additional sales opportunities

**5. Telegram Group (Real-Time Updates)**
- Registrants added to a Telegram group
- Real-time updates as a communication channel
- Pushes from email/SMS redirect here too

**6. Hammer Ads (Retargeting)**
- Retargeting ads to registrants
- Redirect back to confirmation/thank you page
- Upload ~30 ad variations

**7. Morning-Of Engagement**
- Morning question: "What are you hoping to accomplish with the webinar?"
- Creates micro-commitment + engagement

**8. SMS Blast Sequence (Day Of)**
- At least once a month for weekly webinars
- 1 hour before → context text
- 30 min before → context text
- 15 min before → "we're going live" text
- 15 min after start → "here's what we're covering right now"
- 30 min after → contextual update
- Post-webinar → offer text

**9. Post-Webinar Cash Building**
- Full post-webinar process (referenced but detailed in his paid breakdown)
- ManyChat comment trigger: "comment WEBINAR below" for full A-Z process

## Key Principles
- Thank you page is a **conversion hub**, not a dead page
- Interview-style testimonials > selfie videos — one per ICP segment
- VIP upsell pre-webinar = built-in show rate booster + extra revenue
- Real-time communication channel (Telegram/Discord) for push notifications
- SMS cadence on webinar day is aggressive (5+ texts) — justified because they opted in
