## 2. Core Philosophy

### Native Content as Competitive Moat
Ads should not look like ads. They should feel like entertainment. The ad blends into the feed rather than interrupting it. When the creative passes the "is this content or an ad?" test, it bypasses every scroll-past filter.

### Content-First Advertising
Rather than targeting audiences through interest stacks or demographics, Brez moves the targeting upstream into the creative itself. The creative signals who should respond. This predates the mainstream shift to "creative is the new targeting" — he was doing it from day one because TikTok forced it.

### Personal Brand as Lead Gen
Lifestyle content (luxury cars, young entrepreneur aesthetic) serves as top-of-funnel attention mechanism that attracts both clients and students. The personal brand feeds agency AND coaching simultaneously.

## 3. Frameworks

### 3.1 — 3-Stage Funnel

**Stage 1 — TOFU (Top of Funnel)**
Short-form video on TikTok and Instagram. Lifestyle angles, aspiration, identity markers. Purpose: stop the scroll, capture cold attention, build brand recognition.

**Stage 2 — MOFU (Middle of Funnel)**
Long-form nurturing — free training, email guides, community channels, low-ticket offers. Purpose: build trust, qualify, introduce the solution ecosystem.

**Stage 3 — BOFU (Bottom of Funnel)**
Direct conversion: text reminders, follow-up emails, VSLs promoting high-ticket offers. High-touch followup (text + email combined) is notable as a retention/conversion mechanism most agencies skip.

### 3.2 — Creative Approach

- **Primary platform:** TikTok-first → cross-pollinate winners to Meta
- **Creative philosophy:** Entertainment > interruption
- **UGC-style content** that passes the "native" test
- **Viral-first hooks** designed to be shared, not just clicked
- **High-volume testing:** 10-20 new creatives per week
- **Outsourced editing:** Team of VAs and content editors handle production
- **Creative fatigue management:** Constant new production rather than optimization of existing

### 3.3 — Platform Strategy

1. Get native creative working on TikTok (lower CPM, easier testing)
2. Cross-pollinate winning creatives to Meta
3. Scale spend once ROAS confirmed profitable
4. Use email/SMS as backend to protect LTV
5. Testing X (Twitter) ads as diversification play
