### 3.2 — Google Ads: Performance Max Structure

**Critical rule: Brand terms MUST be excluded from Performance Max.**
- Known failure: clients have burned $250K/month by including brand terms in PMax
- Why: Brand terms have high ROAS → PMax optimizes toward them → non-brand budget wasted → new customer acquisition collapses
- When exclusions applied: +5% new customer acquisition, -7% acquisition cost, +31% ROAS improvement

**Campaign architecture:**
- Feed-only PMax campaign = primary shopping campaign (20-40% of total spend)
- Separate branded search campaigns (never mix with non-branded)
- Non-branded search campaigns for intent capture
- YouTube for awareness and retargeting

### 3.3 — Scaling Philosophy

- Scale immediately when new creatives outperform or match existing winners
- Maintain 8-12+ active variations per campaign
- Refresh 25-30% of creative library monthly
- 20-30% of creative production should test outside current winning formula
- New creative duplicates: assign 50-70% of original budget to avoid cannibalization

### 3.4 — Commercial Model

- Performance guarantee: no results = no payment
- No long-term contracts
- No excuses model: if they can't beat the client's current team, client doesn't pay
- Most clients see measurable improvement within 30-60 days
