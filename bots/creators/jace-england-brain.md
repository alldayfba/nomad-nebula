## 2. Core Philosophy & Beliefs

### Standards Theory of Income
Your income is a direct reflection of your standards. If you're "okay" at $10-50K/month, that tolerance IS the ceiling. The fix is not tactics — it is raising what you consider acceptable.

### Risk-Revenue Correlation
The reason you're not scaling is you're not taking enough risk. Not reckless risk — bold investment in advertising, content, and channels.

### Product-Focused Selling (SaaS Mindset)
"Treat your offer like a product-focused SaaS company." The product should sell itself. If you need heavy sales scripts to close, the product/positioning is weak. The call should be Q&A, not pitching.

### Partner, Not Client
"Someone is giving you a percentage of their life's work. This is your partner, not your client." Operators who treat it like a B2C sale will fail. One bad move ruins your rep.

### Mission Over Money
Changed internal desire from chasing record months to building something bigger for the team. "What would scale this client to seven figures?" should be the daily question.

## 3. Named Frameworks & Mechanisms

### The 5-Stage Growth Flywheel (Sequential)

| Stage | Description | When to Deploy |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1** | YouTube funnel + outreach + setter | First (foundation) |
| **Stage 2** | IG content as post-booking sales assets (nurture layer) | Alongside Stage 1 |
| **Stage 3** | Warm pool retargeting via webinars + paid retargeting ads | When Stage 1 converts |
| **Stage 4** | Paid ads on all platforms (boost YouTube, landing pages) | When cash flow is stable |
| **Stage 5** | Referral engine ($50K+ deals from existing clients) | When everything else hums |

**Key rule:** Each stage only activates AFTER the previous one works.

### The Profile Funnel / Flywheel Funnel
Run Meta ads (engagement/traffic objective) to your Instagram profile — NOT to a landing page. The profile IS the funnel. Filled with proof, authority, and value content. Ads generate followers AND revenue simultaneously. Then retarget video viewers with conversion ads.

### The Private VSL (Evergreen Sales Asset)
NOT a traditional VSL on a landing page. A 10-15 minute Loom video explaining the exact benefits of your offer. Sent in DMs and as post-booking required viewing. Three Loom videos per offer covering three core benefits. "The sales letter is your engine."

### Post-Booking Required Viewing System
Between booking and the call, prospects MUST watch:
1. The private VSL / Loom video (required, 10-15 minutes)
2. Case studies
3. YouTube videos (optional but stacked)

Setter confirms they watched before the call proceeds. **If they didn't watch, RESCHEDULE — do not take the call.**

### The Complete Offer Doc
Multi-page document created with AI and voice memos that makes the offer so compelling "your mom, your grandma, everyone can buy it." Built for longevity so even cold audiences convert.

### Two-Variable Bottleneck Analysis
If the system is not converting, there are only two possible issues:
- **(a) Sales team issue** — people are interested but not closing → review 5-10 calls, fix scripts
- **(b) Post-booking/product issue** — people are not interested → fix the Loom video, fix positioning

Nothing else. Binary diagnosis.

### Market Repositioning / Unique Mechanism Naming
Take a saturated market and coin a new term. "TikTok Shop Affiliate Marketing" became "AI Influencer Marketing." Brett Skills went from "SMMA" to "Freelance Brand Scaling." This alone differentiates in crowded niches.

### Telegram Waitlist Launch Sequence
Before building anything, capture leads into a Telegram waitlist group. Give massive value. Launch to the waitlist on a set date. Case study: 1,500 people in waitlist → 300 opt-ins launch day → $100K in 7 days → 125 students enrolled.

### 72-Hour Post-Booking Email Hammer
3-5 emails per day for 72 hours between booking and the call. Via ConvertKit. Combined with required 15-minute Loom. This alone lifts show rates 30%+. Email open rates hit 45-50%+.

### Dial Party Model
Mass power-dialing using Close CRM with smart lists segmented by source. Each rep gets personal local numbers. A2P registration required for SMS. He scaled one offer dialing 170,000+ leads in the first month, booking 4,000+ calls on day one.

## 4. Sales System

### Close Rates & Targets
- Offer owner should close at **50-70%+** on warm leads before hiring closers
- With post-booking system: target **80-90% close rates**
- Show rate target: **75-90%+**
- Split test closers against yourself; when closer hits 60%+, hand off all calls

### Setting Script (DM Sequence)
1. **First message (mutual-based):** "Hey, I see you follow [mutual]. I just scaled X client to $80K/mo and a few others to 7 figures/mo. I think you're sitting on gold. Do you have time for a quick 15-minute call?"
2. **If no reply:** Send the private VSL Loom. "Hey, in case you missed the message, I left a loom video below explaining how I do it."
3. **Follow-up (future-paced):** "Hey bro, I know you can be making $500K/mo. Let me know if this is worth a chat."
4. **Holiday touchpoints:** "Merry Christmas. I can't wait to scale you up. Sorry if I'm being annoying." Always re-attach the Loom. Always push the revenue number.
5. **Comment engagement:** Leave comments on prospect's posts — pulls them into conversation

### Closing Script Structure
Rapport → call framing → goals → context/current situation → bottleneck/opportunity framing → close. But the close should feel like a formality because the Loom video already sold them.

### Close CRM vs GHL
- **Close CRM:** True power dialer (10+ simultaneous), personal numbers per rep, easy call recording, text blasts, smart lists
- **GHL:** "This call is being recorded" disclaimer, not built for power dialing, less efficient

## 5. Ads Strategy

### Profile Ads (Core Strategy)
- **Objective:** Engagement/traffic (NOT conversion)
- **Destination:** Instagram profile (not landing page)
- **Campaign structure:** 1 campaign, ABO, 3 ad sets:
  - Lookalike stack (email optins, profile visitors, closed deals)
  - Interest stack (testing only)
  - Broad (primary — age 22-60 or fully broad)
- **Budget:** Start $10-25/day per ad set for testing. Real results at **$300-500+/day total**
- **Placements:** Manual. Remove Messenger and Audience Network. Keep Instagram + Facebook
- **Per ad set:** 3-5 ads (3 video, 2 static). Can use dynamic

### Two-Bucket Content System (for ads)
Need **30-50 pieces** of short-form content split into:
- **Proof:** Logging into Stripe/Whop, showing results, documenting work, revenue screenshots
- **Authority + Framing:** Teaching something specific, handling objections pre-call, addressing false beliefs

### Retargeting Layer
- Build custom audiences: 50% / 75% / 100% video viewers
- Retarget with "Want to learn how I did X result?" CTA
- Drive to VSL or book-a-call funnel
- Only people who consumed content and built trust see conversion offers

### Results
- One boosted video (134K views) → tracked to **$250-350K in revenue**
- Another (163K views) → **$350K+**
- Best performing ad: **raw screen recording scrolling through payment dashboard, no editing**

## 7. Content Strategy

### YouTube Over VSL
YouTube is a compounding, additive sales asset; a VSL is static.
- You can keep adding videos (adding fuel to fire). A VSL is fixed.
- YouTube ranks in search organically
- YouTube creates massive frame/authority before the sales call
- "I never touch my DMs. The YouTube videos do the work."

### Content Alone Does NOT Retain Clients
Content-only services fail. Must handle both traffic AND conversion. Jace's client lost 4 content-only clients at $3K each because content alone doesn't impact revenue.

### Boring-But-Targeted Content Principle
Videos with 2K views can convert more than videos with 100K views if they attract the right hyper-targeted audience. Niche-specific content filters for qualified buyers.

### Content Cadence Rule
"Every time we post content and run ads, we make money. Every time we don't, we don't."
